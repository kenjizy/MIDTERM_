Mobile Legends: Bang Bang is a popular multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game developed and published by Moonton. Released in 2016, the game quickly gained a large following, particularly in Southeast Asia, where it’s one of the most-played mobile games.

About

Justin Yuan

Justin Yuan is CEO of Moonton Games, best known for "Mobile Legends: Bang Bang." China's ByteDance bought Moonton for approximately $4 billion in 2021. Moonton is headquartered in Shanghai.
